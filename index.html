15 Puzzle

How to Play

Arrange the numbered tiles in order by sliding them into the empty space.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys or Click - Move tiles
  • N - New game
  • P - Pause / Resume
  • F - Focus mode
  • ? - Help
  • T - Themes

Goal

Get tiles 1-15 in order, with the empty space in the bottom-right corner.
